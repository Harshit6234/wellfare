About Us

We believe that every child deserves a fair start in life free from hunger, illness, lack of education, or basic clothing. Founded in 2025, WelfCare is a non-profit digital platform committed to improving the lives of underprivileged children across communities by addressing their most essential needs.

Our mission is built around a simple but powerful vision:
"To ensure that no child is left behind due to poverty or lack of access to basic resources."

We Provide

Food for Children

Providing nutritious meals to underprivileged children across communities.

Health Care for Children

Ensuring access to essential medical services and health education.

Clothes for Children

Delivering warm clothing and school uniforms to children in need.
